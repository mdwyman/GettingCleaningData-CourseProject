Code Book for user_data.txt as produced by run_analysis.R

User: Number of human tester (1-30)

Activity: action of human tester (WALKING, WALKING_UPSTAIRS, WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS, SITTING, STANDING, LAYING)

Average_Body_Acceleration_Magnitude: average of mean for all Body Acceleration Magnitude calculations 

Std_Dev_Body_Acceleration_Magnitude: average of standard deviation for all Body Acceleration Magnitude calculations 

Average_Body_Gyroscopic_Magnitude: average of mean for all Body Gyroscopic Magnitude calculations 

Std_Dev_Body_Gyroscopic_Magnitude: average of standard deviation for all Body Gyroscopic Magnitude calculations
